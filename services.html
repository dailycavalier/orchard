Our services

We are a full-service, wholesale produce company delivering high quality, fresh produce to grocery stores, retail markets, and restaurants throughout Virginia, West Virginia, and the Carolinas.

Dependable Delivery – With our fleet of refrigerated trucks and trained staff friendly delivery drivers, we offer customized delivery schedules to suit your needs. Rain, snow, or shine, we keep our customers’ stocked with fresh produce regardless of any inclement weather Mother Nature sends our way.

Trusted Quality – We source our produce direct from many of the most respected growers both locally and around the globe. Not only do we buy from the best, our state-of-the-art warehouse allows us to keep our produce fresh from the field to our customers’ doors.

Commitment to Customers – “The customer comes first” has always been our attitude and shapes how we do business now more than ever. As a family business, we pride ourselves in offering a personalized customers experience, tailored to your needs by offering things such as customized order/pricing guides and rotating your product when we deliver.
