Our services

We are a full-service, wholesale produce company delivering high quality, fresh produce to grocery stores, retail markets, and restaurants throughout Virginia, West Virginia, and North Carolina.

Dependable Delivery – With our fleet of refrigerated trucks, friendly delivery drivers, and knowledgeable office personnel, we offer customized delivery schedules to suit your needs. Rain, snow, or shine, we keep our customers stocked with fresh produce regardless of any inclement weather Mother Nature sends our way.

Trusted Quality – We source our produce direct from many of the most respected growers both locally and around the globe. Not only do we buy from the best, our state-of-the-art warehouse allows us to keep our produce fresh from the field all the way to our customers’ doors.

Commitment to Customers – "The customer comes first" has always been our attitude and shapes how we do business now more than ever. As a family business, we pride ourselves in providing a personalized customer experience, tailored to your needs by offering customized order/pricing guides and rotating your product when we deliver among other services.
