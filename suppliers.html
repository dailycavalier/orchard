Respected Growers & Shippers

It can be hard to stand out in an industry that is as competitive as wholesale produce, but we let our tireless commitment to shipping only the highest quality produce be a defining factor separating us from the competition. We accomplish this by sourcing our product direct from only the best and most respected growers and shippers in the U.S. We have always made building strong relationships with our suppliers a foundational priority upon which we have built our company. And these strong relationships ensure we are stocked year-round with consistently fresh produce that meets both our high quality and food safety standards.
